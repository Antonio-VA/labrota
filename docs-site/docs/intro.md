LabRota is an AI-powered scheduling platform built for IVF embryology labs. It helps lab coordinators build weekly rotas, manage leave, and keep coverage across departments without manual spreadsheets.

## What you can do in LabRota

- **Generate rotas automatically** — AI builds the weekly schedule based on staff availability, skills, and coverage rules
- **Manage leaves and absences** — track annual leave, sick days, training days, and more with automatic balance tracking
- **View your own schedule** — staff members can log in to see their assigned shifts, swap requests, and upcoming leave
- **Configure your lab** — set shift types, IVF techniques, departments, and coverage ratios to match how your lab works
- **Export to PDF** — share published rotas with your team or print them out

## Roles

| Role | What they can do |
|------|-----------------|
| **Editor / Manager** | Generate rotas, manage staff and leaves, configure the lab |
| **Viewer** | See the schedule, view their own rota, request leave |
