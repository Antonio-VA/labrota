The **Settings** page (Admin) is where organisation-level configuration lives.

## Organisation

- **Name** — your lab or clinic name, shown in PDF exports
- **Logo** — upload a logo that appears on PDF exports (HTTPS URL)
- **Region** — country and region used for public holiday calculations

## Team members & invites

Invite new users by email. Choose their role:

- **Admin / Manager** — full editor access
- **Viewer** — can see the schedule and their own rota only; can submit leave requests if HR mode is on

Invited users receive a magic link by email to set up their account.

## HR settings

- **Annual leave entitlement** — default days per year for new staff
- **Leave requests** — enable to let Viewer accounts submit leave requests for approval
- **Outlook sync** — connect to Microsoft Outlook to sync approved leave to calendar

## Account preferences

Access your personal preferences from the avatar icon (bottom of the sidebar on desktop, or the account icon on mobile):

- **Theme** — Light, Dark, or Auto
- **Accent colour** — choose from preset colours
- **Language** — Spanish, English, or Browser default
