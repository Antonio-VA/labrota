The **Reports** page gives you a monthly overview of coverage, leave, and procedure load across your lab.

## Coverage summary

See how many days in each month met the minimum and optimal coverage thresholds. Days below minimum are highlighted in red.

## Leave summary

A breakdown of leave days taken per staff member per leave type for a selected date range. Useful for year-end HR reporting.

## Procedure load

If punctions data is entered on the schedule, the page shows the total procedure volume per month and how it maps to staffing levels.

## Exporting

Click **Export PDF** to download a printable version of the current view.
