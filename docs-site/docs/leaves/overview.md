The **Leaves** page manages all absences for your team: annual leave, sick days, training, and more.

## Leave types

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Annual | Paid holiday entitlement |
| Sick | Medical absence |
| Training | Off-site courses or conferences |
| Maternity / Paternity | Parental leave |
| Personal | Personal days |
| Unpaid | Leave without pay |
| Other | Any other absence |

## Creating a leave entry

1. Go to **Leaves**
2. Click **Add leave**
3. Select the staff member, leave type, and date range
4. Optionally add notes
5. Save

Approved leave is immediately reflected on the schedule — affected days show a leave indicator on the staff member's chip.

## Status

Leaves can be **Pending**, **Approved**, or **Rejected**. When HR mode is enabled, staff submit requests that go to Pending and require approval. When HR mode is off, all leaves created by editors are automatically approved.

## Leave on the schedule

Staff on leave appear with a leave icon on the schedule grid. The AI engine will not assign them to shifts on those days.
