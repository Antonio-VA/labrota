title: Leave Balance
---

LabRota tracks each staff member's leave entitlement and usage for annual leave (and optionally other types).

## Viewing balances

Open any staff member's profile and click the **Leave** tab to see their current balance:

- **Entitlement** — total days allocated for the year
- **Used** — days already taken
- **Remaining** — entitlement minus used

## Setting entitlements

Editors can set or adjust a staff member's annual leave entitlement from their profile. The default entitlement is configured in **Settings → HR**.

## Overflow leave

If a staff member has used all their annual leave balance, LabRota can automatically draw remaining days from another configured leave type (e.g. a special "extra days" allowance). You'll see a preview of this when creating a leave entry.
