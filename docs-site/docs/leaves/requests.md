title: Leave Requests (HR Mode)
---

When **HR mode** is enabled, staff members can submit leave requests through their own account. Editors review and approve or reject them.

## Enabling HR mode

Go to **Settings → HR** and enable **Leave requests**. This allows staff with Viewer accounts to submit their own leave.

## For staff: submitting a request

1. Log in to LabRota
2. Click **Leaves** in the bottom navigation
3. Tap **Request leave**
4. Choose the leave type, dates, and optionally attach a document
5. Submit — your manager will be notified

You'll see a live preview of your remaining balance before submitting.

## For managers: approving requests

1. Go to **Leaves**
2. Pending requests appear at the top of the list with a yellow indicator
3. Click a request to open it
4. Choose **Approve** or **Reject**

Approved leave immediately appears on the schedule.

## Cancelling leave

Staff can cancel their own pending or approved leave from the Leaves view. Managers can also cancel on their behalf.
