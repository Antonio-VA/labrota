---

The **Team** page lists all staff members in your lab. From here you can add new members, edit existing profiles, and manage onboarding.

## Adding a staff member

1. Click **Add staff** in the top right
2. Fill in the name, role, and email
3. Set the contract type (full-time, part-time, intern)
4. Save — the staff member will appear in the rota immediately

## Roles

- **Lab** — embryologists working in the main lab
- **Andrology** — andrology technicians
- **Admin** — administrative and support staff

Roles determine which shift rows the staff member appears in on the schedule grid.

## Onboarding status

New staff can be marked as **onboarding** with an end date. During onboarding, they count at a reduced weight for coverage calculations. After the end date, they automatically move to full weight.

## Contract types

| Type | Coverage weight |
|------|----------------|
| Full-time | 1.0 |
| Part-time | 0.5 (configurable) |
| Intern | 0.5 (configurable) |

These weights affect how the AI engine calculates whether a day has sufficient coverage.

## Deactivating staff

To remove a staff member from the rota without deleting their history, set their status to **Inactive**. They will no longer appear in new rotas or assignment lists.
